FAQ

Common questions answered

Everything you need to know about selling your home to Properties714.

How fast can you really close?
We can close in as little as 7 days — sometimes faster if both parties are ready. Unlike traditional sales that take 30–90 days, we handle all the paperwork and use a local title company to move quickly. You pick the closing date that works best for you.
Do I need to make repairs before selling?
Absolutely not. We buy homes in any condition — fire damage, water damage, foundation issues, hoarder situations, you name it. We take care of all repairs ourselves after closing. You walk away without spending a single dollar on the property.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero fees, zero commissions. With a traditional real estate agent, you'd pay 5–6% in commissions plus closing costs. When you sell to us, the offer we make is the amount you receive. We cover all standard closing costs.
How is the cash offer calculated?
Our offer is based on the property's current condition, location, comparable sales in the area, and cost of any repairs needed. We aim to make fair offers that work for both parties — we're not looking to lowball, we want to build long-term relationships in the Georgia market.
What if I'm behind on mortgage payments or in foreclosure?
This is one of the most common situations we help with. We can often close before a foreclosure auction date, stopping the process and protecting your credit. Time is critical in these cases — call us immediately at (404) 590-1613 and we'll tell you exactly what's possible.
